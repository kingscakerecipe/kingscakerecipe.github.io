The ingredients needed to cook Gobhi Samosa:
  1. Use 2 cups cauliflower chopped
  2. Take 2 medium sized potatoes cut into small cubes
  3. Take 2 tablespoon peanuts
  4. Get 1 teaspoon panch phoran
  5. Get 1 dry red chilli
  6. You need 1.5 inch ginger crushed
  7. Get 2 green chillies crushed
  8. Get 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  9. Use Salt as required
  10. Prepare Sugar as required
  11. You need 3 cups all purpose flour/maida
  12. Use Vegetable oil as required
Instructions to make Gobhi Samosa:
  1. Heat a pan and dry roast the panch phoran and dry red chilli. Crush them together. Keep it aside.
  2. Heat some oil and add the peanuts. Fry them for a while and keep it aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the cauliflower and little bit of salt. Fry them till light brown in colour. Keep it aside.
  4. Add the potatoes and fry them for a while. Add some salt and again saute for a while.
  5. Add the fried cauliflower, dry roasted masala, crushed ginger and green chillies. Mix it well. Add the peanuts.
  6. Add half cup of water and cover it for a while.
  7. Wait till the oil dries up. Add some sugar to balance the salt. Mix it well and remove it from the flame.
  8. Wait till the stuffing cools down.
  9. In a bowl, add the maida, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup oil.
  10. Mix it well. Add cold water little by little and mix it very well.
  11. Note that it must be a very tight dough.
  12. Cover the dough with a moist cloth for atleast 30mins.
  13. Uncover the batter and make some small balls out of it.
  14. Flatten the balls and add the stuffing in it. Fold it like a samosa, or as given in the picture.
  15. Heat some oil in a wok. Note that you must heat the oil 70%.
  16. Add the samosas and fry them in a medium flame. This will make them crunchy. Fry them in till golden brown in colour.
  17. Serve hot with tea.
