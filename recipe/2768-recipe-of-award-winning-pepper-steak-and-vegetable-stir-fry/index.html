The ingredients needed to cook Pepper steak and vegetable stir fry:
  1. Use olive oil
  2. Take 0,7 kg Steak, cut into thin strips
  3. Take 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  4. Get 1 small onion, half slices
  5. Use 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  6. Get 1 thumb-sized ginger, finally chopped
  7. Take 1 green bell pepper, julienne cut
  8. Use 1 red bell pepper, julienne cut
  9. Take 1 carrot, julienne cut
  10. You need 2 baby morrows, julienne cut
  11. You need Sauce
  12. Take 1 cup water
  13. Get 4 tbls soy sauce
  14. You need 1 tbls sugar
  15. Provide 1 tsp cornstarch
  16. Get 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  17. Prepare 2 tbls worcester sauce
Instructions to make Pepper steak and vegetable stir fry:
  1. Pre heat plate, meduim to high.
  2. Mix together all the sauce ingredients and put one side.
  3. Toss the steak with pepper, until the pepper completely coats the beef.
  4. Add some oil to a pan. Place the beef in and fry until browned.
  5. Put all the veggies in and fry until tender crisp.
  6. Pour the sauce in, bring it to a boil and allow the sauce to thicken.
  7. Serve over a bed of rice, noodles or enjoy just like that.
