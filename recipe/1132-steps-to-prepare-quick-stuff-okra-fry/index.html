The ingredients needed to make Stuff okra fry:
  1. Provide 500 gm okra / bhindi
  2. Get 1 tsp cumin
  3. Take 1 tsp fennel
  4. Take 1 tsp whole spice powder
  5. Use 1 tsp dry mango powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli
  7. Prepare as per need salt
  8. Take 1 pinch asafoetida
  9. Prepare 1 tsp turmeric
  10. You need 2 tbsp coriander powder
  11. Take 2 tbsp or more if needed mustard oil
Steps to make Stuff okra fry:
  1. First wash okra and keep aside for dry
  2. Now in a bowl mix all spices
  3. Now slit okras and fill this stuffing in it
  4. Now in a pan heat oil add carom seeds and fry all okra untill it's done tender and cut easily,serve with paratha or chapati and rice.
