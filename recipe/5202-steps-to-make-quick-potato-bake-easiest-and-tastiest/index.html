The ingredients needed to prepare Potato Bake. (Easiest and Tastiest):
  1. Take 6 large Potatoes pealed
  2. Provide 1 clove garlic crushed (optional)
  3. Get 1 Packet brown onion power soup mix (50 grams)
  4. You need 250 ml Fresh cream
  5. Prepare 100 ml Full cream milk
  6. Prepare 1 Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions to make Potato Bake. (Easiest and Tastiest):
  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Slice potatoes, about 10mm thick.
  3. Place in a greased oven dish. (440mm x 440mm and 60mm deep)
  4. Mix the remaining ingredients together and pour over potatoes.
  5. Bake for 1 hour or until potatoes are golden brown and soft.
  6. Remove from oven and rest for 10 minutes. Enjoy.
