The ingredients needed to cook Olive oil fried egg and hummus toast:
  1. Provide 1 whole wheat brown bread
  2. Take 1 egg
  3. Use 100 gm chickpeas
  4. Provide 3-4 Tbsp olive oil
  5. Take 1/2 tsp paprika
  6. Prepare 1 tsp lime juice
  7. Take 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  8. Provide 1 garlic pod chopped fine
  9. Use To taste Salt
Instructions to make Olive oil fried egg and hummus toast:
  1. Wash and soak chickpeas in a bowl. Don’t discard the water. Separate the chickpeas and set aside.
  2. In a food processor add lime juice to the chickpeas, cumin powder, chopped garlic, olive oil, salt and tahini sauce. Add 1-3 Tbsp chickpea soaked water in the food processor
  3. Run the food processor until the hummus is smooth
  4. In a non stick pan add olive oil and break the egg to make a fried egg.
  5. Apply hummus on the toasted whole wheat bread and add the olive oil fried egg on it. Enjoy with a cup of tea.
