The ingredients needed to make Banana walnut oats cake:
  1. Get bananas
  2. Prepare brown sugar
  3. Use oats
  4. Take wheat flour
  5. Provide oil/ghee
  6. Prepare baking powder
  7. Use baking soda
  8. Get salt
  9. Get cinnamon powder
  10. Use chopped walnuts
  11. Take choco chips
  12. Provide vinegar
  13. Take milk
Instructions to make Banana walnut oats cake:
  1. First mash the bananas in a mixing bowl. It is good if the bananas are a little over ripen
  2. Now add brown sugar and oil and mix well till the mixture is well combined
  3. Keep the oven to Pre heat at 160c for 10mins.. Now add milk and mix well
  4. Then seive wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon powder and salt for 2-3 times and add to the wet mixture
  5. Add in oats and mix well.. Then add walnuts and choco chips and mix gently.
  6. Grease the cake tin and keep ready. Finally add vinegar mix well and pour into the greased tin top with some nuts and choco chips and tap a couple of times..
  7. Now bake in preheated oven at 160c for 25-30 mins or untill done. Check by inserting a toothpick in the center to come out clean
