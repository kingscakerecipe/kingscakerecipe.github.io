The ingredients needed to make Chocolate pancakes:
  1. You need 100 gm all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Use Pinch salt
  4. Use 1 egg
  5. You need 10 gm melted butter
  6. Get 100 ml milk
  7. Use 3 tbsp chocolate spread
  8. Provide 3 tbsp chopped/ grated chocolate
  9. Use 3 tbsp castor sugar
  10. Take 1 apple
Instructions to make Chocolate pancakes:
  1. Take flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Make a hole in middle and add egg and melted butter and then milk. Whisk them all well till lump free.
  3. Heat a non stick pan and put some oil and butter. Spread the pancake mix and add 1 tbsp chocolate spread and 1 tbsp chopped chocolate in middle. Cover it with more batter.
  4. Flip over the pancake when done from under side and let the other side cook too. Similarly make more pancakes.
  5. Put some castor sugar in another pan and let it caramelise. Add peeled and sliced apples and let them coat well.
  6. Serve the pancakes hot with or without caramelised fruit.
