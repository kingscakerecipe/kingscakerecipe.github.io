The ingredients needed to make Beguni (Egg Plant Fritters):
  1. Get 1medium sized Egg plant
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoon Gram flour
  3. Prepare 2 tablespoon Rice flour
  4. Take 1/2 teaspoon Ginger & Garlic paste
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Red chilli powder
  7. You need 1/2 teaspoon Panch Phoran/ Five Blisters powder
  8. Use 1/2 teaspoon Black Cumin
  9. Provide 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder
  10. Provide As needed Water
  11. You need 1.5 cup Oil
  12. Prepare to taste Salt
Instructions to make Beguni (Egg Plant Fritters):
  1. Wash the egg plant & cut it into thin slices.
  2. Except egg plant, oil & water take all the ingredients in a bowl & mix the dry ingredients well. Now slowly add water to it & make a thick batter.
  3. Take a deep frying pan in the gas, add oil to it, when the oil is heated, lower the flame. Now rub some salt & black pepper powder in the egg plant slices, then dip the slices in the batter, slowly put the slices one by one & deep fry them until they have turned golden brown. Absorb the extra oil using a kitchen tissue & your Beguni is ready to serve! Enjoy it with your favourite dip or chutney.
