The ingredients needed to make Panettone French Toast:
  1. Use 2 eggs, beaten
  2. Use 4 slices panettone
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  4. Use 4 tbsp granulated sugar
  5. Get 50 ml whole milk
  6. Provide Vanilla ice cream
  7. Use vanilla custard
  8. Take 2 knobs butter
Steps to make Panettone French Toast:
  1. Mix the eggs and milk together in a shallow bowl.
  2. In a frying pan over a low to medium heat melt the butter.
  3. Soak the panettone slices on each side in the egg mixture.
  4. Add the slices to the hot pan as you go along and fry on each side for 2 minutes.
  5. Mix the cinnamon and sugar together well in a small bowl.
  6. Remove the panettone from the pan and transfer to a bowl.
  7. Sprinkle a tablespoon of the sugar mixture to the slices.
  8. Drizzle with vanilla custard and a scope of vanilla ice cream.
  9. Serve when ready.
