The ingredients needed to cook KADHA - The traditional immune boosting drink:
  1. Get 1 bay leaf
  2. Get 1 tbsp grated ginger
  3. You need 2 cloves
  4. Get 8-10 Tulsi leaves
  5. Provide As per taste Palm sugar candy/ honey
  6. Prepare As per taste Black salt/pink salt
  7. Get 1 tsp Tea powder (optional)
  8. Get Few Lemon slices
  9. Provide 1 tsp Crushed peppercorns
  10. Use As required Water
Instructions to make KADHA - The traditional immune boosting drink:
  1. In a pan add water, 1 bay leaf, grated ginger, cloves, crushed black peppercorns and tulsi leaves and let it boil till water reduces to half.
  2. Now add in palm sugar candy,black salt/pink salt, little tea(optional) and let it boil for 5 more minutes.
  3. Strain the mixture in glass add lemon juice/sliced lemon or drizzle few drops of honey (if not adding palm sugar) before drinking and sip it slowly.
