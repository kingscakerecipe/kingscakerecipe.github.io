The ingredients needed to prepare Pan pizza:
  1. You need pizza dough ingredients!
  2. Use 2 cup All purpose flour
  3. Get Salt 2tsp or as required
  4. Get 2 tsp Yeast
  5. Provide 1 pinch Sugar
  6. Provide 2 tsp Butter
  7. You need Milk as required(for knead)
  8. Take Chicken marination ingredients!
  9. Prepare Chicken boneless as you required
  10. Get Salt 2tps
  11. Take Paprika powder 1tps
  12. Provide Red chilli powder 1tps
  13. Take Coriander powder 1tps
  14. Prepare Black pepper 1tps
  15. Use Soy sauce 1tps
  16. Use Vinegar 1tps
  17. You need Chilli sauce 1tps
  18. You need Oyster sauce 1tps
  19. Take Worcestershire sauce 1tsp!!!!
  20. Take (2 tbs) Oil for making chicken
  21. You need Pizza sauce for spreading on dough
  22. Use 2 pinch Oregano
  23. Take Olive (optional) as you required
  24. Get Mushrooms (optional) as you required
  25. Take Onion (optional) as you required
  26. Prepare Mozerella cheese (half cup) grated
  27. Get Cheedar cheese (half cup) grated
Steps to make Pan pizza:
  1. Put all the dough ingredients in a bowl and knead it for five min and rest it for 2 hour until rise.
  2. In a pan add 2 tbs of oil. Put all marinated chicken in pan and cook for 8 to 10 minutes until it's done completely
  3. Now make a round pizza shape on the pan. And turn on the flame.
  4. Put pizza sauce on the dough. And spread chicken on the dough.put some mozerella and cheddar cheese. Put some onion on it. Put some oregano in it. Put some red chilli crushed in it(optional) put some mushrooms (optional) put some olives (optional) in it.
  5. And bake it for 10 to 15 minutes. Now pizza is ready for eating. Serve pizza with ketchup.
