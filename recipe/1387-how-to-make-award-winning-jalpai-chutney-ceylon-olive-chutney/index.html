The ingredients needed to cook Jalpai Chutney (Ceylon Olive Chutney):
  1. Use 500 gm Jalpai (Ceylon Olive)
  2. Get 1 dry red chilli
  3. Use 500 gm sugar
  4. You need 1 teaspoon salt
  5. You need 1 teaspoon mustard oil
  6. Use Roasted masala:
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  9. Use 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
Steps to make Jalpai Chutney (Ceylon Olive Chutney):
  1. Heat a wok and dry roast all the ingredients written under roasted masala and grind it to a smooth powder.
  2. Peel the Jalpai and boil it in a container by adding some water.
  3. When it become soft, remove it from the flame and remove the water.
  4. Smash the Jalpai altogether and remove the seeds from inside.
  5. Heat the oil in a wok, add the dry red chilli, saute it for a while and then add the smashed Jalpai.
  6. Add the salt and the sugar and add 1/2 cup of water. Mix it very well.
  7. When you get the desired thick consistency, add the roasted masala and mix it well.
  8. Remove it from the flame, serve chilled.
