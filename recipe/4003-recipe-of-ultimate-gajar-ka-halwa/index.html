The ingredients needed to prepare Gajar ka halwa:
  1. Provide 1/2 kg or 500gm carrots
  2. Use 1/2 lit milk
  3. Provide To taste Sugar
  4. Provide 1 tsp crushed green elaichi / cardamom
  5. Use 1 palm palm full of dry fruits as per your choice
  6. Prepare 5-7 tsp milk powder (optional)
  7. You need 2 tbsp ghee
Steps to make Gajar ka halwa:
  1. First boil the Milk in a thick bottomed pan and after boiling milk lower the flame
  2. On other side peel wash and grate the carrots
  3. Now heat kadai and pour grated carrots in it and saute
  4. When carrots started changing colour and leaving the side of kadai add milk, sugar and add milk powder and continuously stirring
  5. Stir till all the milk dried up completely
  6. Now add chopped dry fruits, crushed elaichi, ghee, stir till you see mixture starts leaving the sides of kadai
  7. Garnish with dry dry fruits of your choice, serve hot or chill as your choice
