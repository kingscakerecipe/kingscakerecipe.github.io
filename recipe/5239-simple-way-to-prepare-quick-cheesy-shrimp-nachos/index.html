The ingredients needed to prepare Cheesy Shrimp Nachos:
  1. Get Tortilla chips (sometimes I make my own out of corn tortillas)
  2. You need Cheese (I used sharp cheddar)
  3. Use 2/3 dozen Shrimp (I used the frozen, pre-cooked kind)
  4. Take Garlic Sea Salt
  5. Provide Sourcream
  6. Prepare Guacamole
  7. Provide Mild to Medium salsa
Steps to make Cheesy Shrimp Nachos:
  1. Shred cheese if it isn't already shredded.
  2. Layer chips and cheese to your liking. Sprinkle Garlic Sea Salt if you wish.
  3. Thaw, and cut or rip shrimp to your liking and place on top of chips and cheese.
  4. Microwave for about 1 minute and 20 seconds or until cheese is to your melted preference.
  5. Top with your desired toppings.
