The ingredients needed to prepare Oats Dry Fruit Laddu:
  1. Get oats
  2. Prepare jaggery
  3. Get green cardamom powder
  4. Use almonds
  5. Take cashew nut
  6. You need raisins
  7. Provide pistachios
  8. Use ghee
Instructions to make Oats Dry Fruit Laddu:
  1. In a pan heat 2 tsp of ghee. Add two cup of oats. Roast it for 3-4 minutes on medium flame. Then switch the flame off. Blend the roasted oats in a blender jar.
  2. Now heat a pan add 2 tsp of ghee. Add all dry fruits. Cook for 1-2 minutes on medium flame. Take it out from the pan. Then coarsely grind the dry fruits.
  3. In the same pan add one cup jaggery. Melt it on low flame. Now switch the flame off.
  4. In the melted jaggery add powdered oats, coarsely ground dry fruits, cardamom powder and mix well. Let it cool. Add 2-3 tbsp of ghee. Mix it form a dough.
  5. Take out a small portion from the dough, roll it between your palms and make laddu. Make all the laddus in the similar manner.
