The ingredients needed to prepare Onion ang garlic crescent rolls:
  1. Use 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Provide 3/4 teaspoon instant yeast
  3. You need 1/2 cup warm water
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup sugar
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 1 large eggs
  7. Get 1/8 cup onion, finely chopped
  8. Get 1/2 tablespoon garlic, minced
  9. You need 1/2 cup butter, room temperature
  10. Provide Egg wash
  11. Take Sesame seeds (optional)
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup butter, softened (for brushing)
Instructions to make Onion ang garlic crescent rolls:
  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water. - - Stir in sugar, salt, eggs, butter, onion and 1 cups of flour. Beat until smooth. Mix in remaining flour until smooth. Scrape dough from side of bowl. Knead dough, then cover it and let rise in a warm place until double.
  2. Mix the 1/4 cup butter with garlic and set a side
  3. Punch down dough. Divide into 4 balls and roll them. Brush with the butter mixture and stack them.
  4. Roll out the stacked dough into a 12-inch circle and spread butter.
  5. Cut into 10 to 15 wedge. Roll up the wedges starting with the wide end.
  6. Place rolls with point under on a greased baking sheet. Cover and let rise until double (about 1 hour).
  7. Apply egg wash on the rolls and sprinkle sesame seeds. Bake at 205C for 12-15 minute or until golden brown.
