  2. Provide 3 tbsp corn flour
  3. Provide 3 tbsp sugar
  4. Get 1 packet instant orange jelly
  5. Use 2 cup cakes
Steps to make Orange tiramisu:
  1. Heat milk add sugar
  2. Reserve 1/2 cup milk add corn flour in it
  3. Now wen milk boils add cornflour mix
  4. After custard thickens
  5. Turn off flame and let it cool down
