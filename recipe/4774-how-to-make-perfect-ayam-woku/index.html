The ingredients needed to cook Ayam Woku:
  1. Provide 4 skin-on chicken legs
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  3. You need 5 kaffir lime leaves
  4. Prepare 2 pandan leaves, knotted
  5. Take 2 lemongrass, bruised and knotted
  6. Provide 2 tsp salt
  7. Take 2 tsp sugar
  8. Provide 750 ml water
  9. Prepare 1 tomato, cut into small pieces
  10. Get 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  11. Provide 1/4 cup lemon basil leaves or thai basil
  12. Get Spice Paste :
  13. Use 5 shallots
  14. Provide 3 cloves garlic
  15. Prepare 3 red big chilies
  16. Get 5 red bird eye chilies
  17. Provide 5 candlenuts
  18. Get 1 /2 tsp ground turmeric
  19. You need 2 inch ginger
Steps to make Ayam Woku:
  1. Grind all the spice paste ingredients using a food processor. Put a bit of oil to loosen the texture. Put aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pot. Sauté the spice paste, kaffir lime leaves, pandan leaves and lemongrass until fragrant. About 4 to 5 minutes.
  3. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  4. Add salt, sugar and water. Bring to boil and continue cooking until the chicken are fully cooked. Adjust salt and sugar as needed.
  5. Add tomato, sliced spring onions and lemon basil. Stir and cook until lemon basil are wilted.
  6. Turn off the heat, transfer to a serving bowl and serve with rice.
