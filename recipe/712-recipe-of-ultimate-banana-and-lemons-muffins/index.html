The ingredients needed to cook Banana and lemons muffins:
  1. You need 3 cups flour
  2. Provide 1 Tablespoon baking powder
  3. You need Pinch salt
  4. You need 1/2 cup cooking oil
  5. Take 5 bananas
  6. You need 1/2 lemon and the zest
  7. Use 1 cup sugar
  8. Provide 2 eggs
Instructions to make Banana and lemons muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 180'c
  2. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt add lemon zest
  3. Mashed the bananas add lemon juice,sugar,cooking oil and the eggs and mix
  4. Combine the dry ingredients with wet ingredients
  5. Put the mixture into muffins pan and bake for 25-30 minutes and enjoy
