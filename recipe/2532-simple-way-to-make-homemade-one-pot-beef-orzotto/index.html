The ingredients needed to make One pot beef orzotto:
  1. Prepare 250 g beef mince
  2. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  3. Take 2 carrots, grated
  4. Take 150 ml orzo pasta
  5. Get 4 tbs tomato puré
  6. Prepare 1 beef stock cube
  7. Get 300 ml water
  8. Prepare 1 tsp dried basil
  9. Prepare Topping
  10. Prepare 100 ml panko
  11. Prepare 100 ml grated parmesan
Instructions to make One pot beef orzotto:
  1. Fry beef mince and add onion and carrots. Fry until onions are soft, then add tomato puré and fry for a bit longer.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and let simmer until pasta is done. Add more water if it gets too dry.
  3. Mix panko and cheese and sprinkle it over the dish. Bake in the oven at 225 degrees until golden. Enjoy!
