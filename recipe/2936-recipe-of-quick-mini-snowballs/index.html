The ingredients needed to prepare Mini Snowballs:
  1. Provide 625 ml self-raising flour
  2. Get 125 g butter or margarine
  3. Provide 250 ml castor sugar
  4. Get 5 ml vanilla essence
  5. Prepare 125 ml smooth apricot jam plus extra 45ml fir spreading
  6. Use 125 ml water
  7. Use 1 cup desiccated coconut
  8. Take 125 ml sour milk
  9. Take Red food colouring
Instructions to make Mini Snowballs:
  1. Soft the flour into a large bowl. Rub in the butter until the mixture resembles fine bread crumbs. Add castor sugar
  2. In another bowl, combine the sour milk and essence, then add to the dry mixture until a soft dough has formed
  3. Roll into 24 balls(I decide to make 12 giant balls this time) and place on a greased baking tray. Flatten slightly using a fork. Bake at 180 °C for 15-20 minutes. Leave to cool
  4. Heat the jam and water together to make a syrup. Spread eahals with the extra jam and sandwich together then dunk into the warm syrup
  5. Colour the coconut with red food colouring. Roll the snowball in the coconut and leave to cool. Enjoy!…
