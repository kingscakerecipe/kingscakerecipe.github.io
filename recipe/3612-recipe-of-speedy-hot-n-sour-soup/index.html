The ingredients needed to cook Hot n Sour Soup:
  1. You need 1 cup capsicum cut into thin rectangles
  2. Take 1 cup onions cut into large pieces
  3. Provide 1 tbsp chopped ginger
  4. You need 1 tbsp chopped green chillies
  5. You need 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  6. Provide 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  7. Take 1 tbsp mushroom oyster sauce (or 1 tbsp light soy sauce)
  8. Provide 1 tsp green chilli sauce
  9. Prepare 10-12 freshly crushed peppercorn
  10. Provide Water as required
  11. Prepare to taste Salt
  12. Use Oil as required
  13. Take Corn flour as required
Steps to make Hot n Sour Soup:
  1. In the same pan add another tbsp of oil add ginger and green chillies.
  2. Sauté for minute now add the onion and fry on high flame for another minute.
  3. Add all the sauces,salt remaining pepper powder and water. Roughly around 1/2 cup.
  4. Add the Paneer and bring it to a boil.
  5. Add 1 tbsp of corn flour slurry and let the sauce thicken. Serve pipping hot with rice
