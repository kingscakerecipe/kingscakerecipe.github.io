The ingredients needed to cook Masala okra (bhindi):
  1. Get 250 grams Okra (bhindi) cut in small pieces
  2. Take 4-5 sliced big onions
  3. Provide Some baby onions having cut on them
  4. You need to taste Salt
  5. You need 1 tbsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  6. Provide to taste Chopped green chilli acc
  7. Prepare 1/2 tbsp coriander powder
  8. Get 1/2 tbsp cumin powder
  9. Use 1/2 tbsp garam masala
  10. Get 1 tbsp dry mango powder
  11. Get 2 tbsp oil
Steps to make Masala okra (bhindi):
  1. Take a pan. Add oil heat it well. Add okra (bhindi). Saute for 2 to 3 mnts uncovered.
  2. Then add sliced onions and mix them well saute for 2 mnts. Then add baby onions and saute them for 2 to 3 mnts.
  3. Then add all spices one by one.
  4. Cook till turns crispy..cook it uncovered if u want nonsticky flavour.
  5. In last add dry mango powder.mix well and switch off the gas. If u want some more tangyness than add some drops of lemon juice
