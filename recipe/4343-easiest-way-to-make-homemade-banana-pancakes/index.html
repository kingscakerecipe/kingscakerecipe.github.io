The ingredients needed to prepare Banana pancakes:
  1. Prepare 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Provide 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Use 1 1/2 ripe bananas
  5. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Take 4 tbsp sugar
  7. Use 1/2 cup milk
Steps to make Banana pancakes:
  1. Mix flour n baking powder
  2. Peel the ripe bananas,cut and put in the blender,add the eggs then blend
  3. Add milk,sugar n vanilla essence in that blender and blend all of them in a blender
  4. Pour that mixture into the flour,mix thoroughly using a whisk or wooden spoon until it becomes consistence
  5. Put your chapati pan of any other pan on heat n let it become hot then reduce the heat,apply oil on it then pour your batter from the centre as it spread
  6. Let it form pores on topthen apply little oil then turn it and fry both sides until it becomes golden brown.
  7. Repeat the above procedure until you are done with your batter.
