The ingredients needed to make My Peri-Peri Prawns:
  1. Prepare 800 g prawns
  2. Get 1/4 cup peri-peri sauce
  3. Take Bunch fresh parsley
  4. Take 1 small lemon
  5. You need 3 cloves garlic, minced
  6. Take Salt
  7. Get Pepper
  8. You need 2 Tablespoons Olive oil
Steps to make My Peri-Peri Prawns:
  1. Clean and devein the prawns.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix prawns, fresh parsley, chopped garlic and juice of half a lemon. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heal olive oil in pan. Add prawns and saut√© until prawns are pink almost all the way through.
  4. Add peri peri sauce to pan and cook another 2-3 minutes. Cut a prawn through the centre to see if it is opaque all the way through, otherwise cook another minute or two. Cooking times vary according to the size of the prawns. Squeeze over the juice of the remaining half of the lemon and season to taste. Serve with basmati rice and a fresh salad.
