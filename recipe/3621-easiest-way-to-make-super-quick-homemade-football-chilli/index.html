The ingredients needed to cook Football Chilli:
  1. Get 4.5 lbs groundbeef
  2. Get 2 tablespoons Seasoning of choice(Dale's seasonings)
  3. You need 1 can petite diced tomatoes
  4. Get 1 can tomato sauce
  5. Provide 1 can roasted corn
  6. Get 2 large can of tomatoe juice
  7. Provide 6 packets Chilli powder
  8. Use 1 can black beans
  9. Prepare 1 can chilli beans
  10. Get 1 can kidney beans
  11. Prepare 1 small onion diced petite and sauteed
  12. Provide Toppings to add to the chilli
  13. Get Shredded Cheese
  14. Provide Jalapeno peppers
  15. Get Hot sauce
  16. You need Sour cream
  17. Take Spaghetti
  18. Prepare 1 batch cornbread
  19. Provide Petite diced onions
  20. Take Tortilla chips
  21. Provide Oyster Crackers
Instructions to make Football Chilli:
  1. In skillet fry the ground beef.(I use Dale's seasoning or you can use my recipe I make when I run out of Dale's) Season with my substitute version of Dale's, or dale's seasoning picture below. In a real pinch use Worcestershire, steak seasoning (I use longhorn).
  2. Saute the onions in butter in the same pan that you fried the groundbeef in til desired doneiness.
  3. In large pot add the juice, sauce, beans, chilli powder, petite tomatoes, corn, cooked groundbeef and the sauteed onions. Let simmer for 1/2 hr.
  4. Ladle up a bowl of chilli. Top with toppings of choice (I like cornbread, cheese, sour cream and hot sauce). Enjoy.
