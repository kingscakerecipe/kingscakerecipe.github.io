The ingredients needed to make Pancakes:
  1. Use 1 cup Milk
  2. You need 2 tbsp Oil
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Get 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  5. You need 1/2 tsp Salt
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp Sugar
  7. Provide 2 tsp Baking Powder
Instructions to make Pancakes:
  1. In a bowl, whisk together milk, oil and egg. Combine the dry ingredients and add to the egg mixture. Whisk until the batter is smooth.
  2. Grease a pan lightly with butter and heat over medium. Scoop the batter on to the pan, using 1/4 cup for each pancake. Heat until pancake bubbles and flip, about 2-3 minutes for each side, until lightly golden brown.
  3. Continue making pancakes with the rest of the batter.
  4. Enjoy it with honey or maple syrup.
  5. Or just cut them into small pieces and add it to a bowl. Top it with mango cream, pulped mangoes and mango pieces and serve.
