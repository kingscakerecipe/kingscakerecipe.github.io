Instructions to make Crispy fried chicken and pizza fries:
  1. At first chopped the chicken flay
  2. Then add chilli garlic sauce, chilli sauce, soya sauce, whoster sauce
  3. Then add black and white pepper
  4. Then red chilli powder and mustard powder
  5. Then salt and marinate for few hours of for overnight… Then for coating add red chilli flakes and cornflour in the egg and beat it well
  6. Then add spring onion in broast crumb and then start the coating first dip chicken in egg then flour
  7. Then in broast crumbs properly then deep fry on low flame so that it will tenderize and become crispy
  8. Chicken is ready… Now coat the fries with above mentioned ingredients for 10 to 15 mints the deep fry them
  9. Now add chat masala, black pepper, salt and chicken powder in fries and mix along with this prepare coleslaw
  10. Then plate then add layer of fries then chilli garlic sauce then garnish capsicum, spring onions, tomatoes then coleslaw then add fried chicken then add cheddar cheese over it
  11. Then serve….
