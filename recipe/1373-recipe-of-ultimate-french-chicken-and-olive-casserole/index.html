The ingredients needed to cook French chicken and olive casserole:
  1. Take Small bunch broccoli cut
  2. Get Small bunch Curliflower cut
  3. Provide 1 stalk leek diced
  4. Provide Corn on the cob cut
  5. You need 5 Irish potatoes cut in twos
  6. Prepare Parsley shredded
  7. Take 1/2 tsp Rosemary
  8. You need White wine 2 caps
  9. Take 1 cup Chicken stock
  10. You need 2 Seasonings
  11. Get 1/2 tsp Sea salt
  12. Take 1/2 tsp Cumin
  13. You need Carrots diced
  14. Use Onions sliced
  15. Use Cucumber sliced
  16. Use 1 tsp Curry
  17. Get 1 tsp Black pepper
  18. Provide Red capsicum diced
  19. Provide Tomatoes chopped
  20. Get Olive oil
  21. Take Yellow capsicum diced
  22. Provide Garlic crushed
  23. Get Half cup olives
Instructions to make French chicken and olive casserole:
  1. Heat up the pan and add the olive oil
  2. Add the onions,garlic, pepper and tomatoes and rosemary,stir fry for few minutes until a little bit brown
  3. Add the chicken stock,corn,Irish potatoes,white wine,curry,cumin,black pepper And seasoning
  4. Cover and all to cook for ten minuets
  5. Add the broccoli,carrots,curliflower and olives.
  6. Stir and cook for a few minutes
  7. Then add the parsley,carrots and cucumber last
  8. Serve hot and use and beef or chicken as the garnishing
