The ingredients needed to prepare Oats Cheese Veg Frankie:
  1. Take Oats
  2. Prepare wheat flour
  3. Provide cottage cheese
  4. Use medium carrot cut in julians
  5. Use capsicum cut in julians
  6. You need chopped onion
  7. Get finely chopped tomato
  8. You need tomato ketchup
  9. Provide Mayonnaise
  10. Provide salt
  11. Take black pepper powder
  12. You need chat masala
  13. You need Oil
  14. Use Ghee for shallow frying
Instructions to make Oats Cheese Veg Frankie:
  1. Heat a pan. Dry roast oats in it for two minutes.
  2. Transfer these oats in a grinder jar and grind it in to a fine powder.
  3. In a mixing bowl add wheat flour and oats powder and knead a soft dough by using water. Keep aside for 15 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a pan.
  5. Add onions in it and saute for two minutes.
  6. Add tomatoes in it and saute for five minutes.
  7. Add carrot and capsicum in it and saute for two minutes.
  8. Add salt, pepper and chat masala in it. Mix well.
  9. Add paneer cubes in it. Mix well. Its ready.
  10. Take a small ball from dough and dust with wheat flour and roll it with rolling pin.
  11. Heat a tawa. Place roti on it.
  12. Flip off after one minute and cook it until done.
  13. Spread tomato sauce on chapati.
  14. Place veg stuffing on it.
  15. Pour some mayonese on it. Roll it carefully.
  16. Put some ghee on tawa.
  17. Shellow fry it from both sides.
  18. Its ready

This street food is originated from Kolkata. It is very simple and easy to make. Veg frankie has a delicious and tangy pattice of vegetable rolled with some salad in a chapati. It is a popular street food. Frankie is comfort food and a great tiffin or lunch box option.
