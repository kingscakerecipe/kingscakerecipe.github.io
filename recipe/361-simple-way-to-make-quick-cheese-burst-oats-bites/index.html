The ingredients needed to make Cheese burst oats bites:
  1. Take bread
  2. Get Besan
  3. Provide Mozzarella cheese
  4. Get Spices
  5. Take Haldi pinch
  6. Take Red chilli as per taste
  7. You need Salt as per taste
  8. Use chat masala
  9. Take Sauce green chutney
  10. Get Oil for deep fry
  11. Take Oats for coating
Instructions to make Cheese burst oats bites:
  1. Make thick batter of besan
  2. Add all spices in batter
  3. Take 2slices of bread
  4. Apply green chutney,and sauce as cheese on it
  5. Keep one slice on other and cut it in square.
  6. Dip it in batter
  7. Roll on oats
  8. Deep fry it
  9. Cut/Garnish and serve
