The ingredients needed to make Chicken chow mein:
  1. You need 1/2 packet spaghetti
  2. You need 250 gram chicken (julian cut)
  3. Prepare 1 medium capsicum
  4. You need 1 medium carrot
  5. Provide 1/4 piece cabbage
  6. Prepare 1 medium spring onion (only green pary)
  7. Use 5 cloves garlic
  8. You need 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  9. Take 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  10. Use 2 tbsp soya sauce
  11. Take 2 tbsp chilli sauce
  12. Use 1 tbsp lemon juice
  13. Take 1 tsp chicken powder
  14. You need 1/2 tsp white pepper
  15. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
  16. Use to taste Salt
  17. You need 2 tbsp oil
Instructions to make Chicken chow mein:
  1. Place all the ingredients on kitchen counter. Cut chicken & vegetables in julian form.
  2. In a pan add water let it boil. Then in boiling water add half packet spaghetti & boil not overcook. Then drain cold water on it & set a side.
  3. On other side in a pan add oil garlic & little bit fry the garlic then add chicken & cook well.
  4. When is completely cooked add vegetables and cook for only 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add worcestershire sauce, oyster sauce & soya sauce.
  6. Add chilli sauce, lemon juice & white pepper.
  7. Add black pepper, salt & chicken powder.
  8. Mis well & add spaghetti again mix well.
  9. Chicken chow mein is ready to serve.
