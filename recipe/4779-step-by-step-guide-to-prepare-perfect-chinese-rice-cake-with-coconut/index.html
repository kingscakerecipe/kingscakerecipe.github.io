The ingredients needed to cook Chinese rice cake with coconut:
  1. You need 200 gr Chinese rice cake
  2. Provide 100 gr shredded coconut
  3. Take 1 pinch salt
  4. Take 1 Pandan leaves, washed and dreid then tied a into a knot
Instructions to make Chinese rice cake with coconut:
  1. Cut the room temperature rice cake into bite size
  2. Steamed the shreded coconut with pandan and add salt, mix well
  3. Slowly roll the rice cake onto the plate of shredded coconut and serve
