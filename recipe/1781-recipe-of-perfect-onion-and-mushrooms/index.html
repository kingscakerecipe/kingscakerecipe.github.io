The ingredients needed to prepare Onion and Mushrooms:
  1. Prepare 2 extra-large onions, sliced
  2. Take 8 oz white mushrooms
  3. You need 1/2 stick butter
  4. Get 1 Tbs jarred minced garlic
  5. Get 1 Tbs Worcestershire sauce
Instructions to make Onion and Mushrooms:
  1. Cut onions in half and then slice them. Slice mushrooms if you bought whole mushrooms.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter, add in onions and minced garlic. Cook on medium-low heat for 15 minutes. Add in mushrooms and Worcestershire sauce, continue cooking for 15 minutes.
