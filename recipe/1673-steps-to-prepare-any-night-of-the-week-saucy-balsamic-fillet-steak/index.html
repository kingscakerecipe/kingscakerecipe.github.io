The ingredients needed to make Saucy Balsamic Fillet Steak:
  1. Get 1 kg fillet steak sliced thick
  2. You need 1 tsp cumin
  3. Get 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  4. Take 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  5. You need juice of 3 limes
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp crushed garlic
  8. Provide 1 tsp dry rosemary
  9. Get 1 onion sliced
  10. Take 2 tomatoes sliced thick
  11. Prepare 50 g butter
Instructions to make Saucy Balsamic Fillet Steak:
  1. Slice the fillet steak
  2. Add all ingredients into the pot and marinate the steak.
  3. Cover with sliced onions and tomatoes. top with butter
  4. Allow to cook on slow heat for 30 mins. the steak is ready when the onions and tomatoes make a sauce and the steak is juicy and soft
