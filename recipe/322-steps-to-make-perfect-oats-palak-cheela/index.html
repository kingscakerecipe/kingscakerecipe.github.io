The ingredients needed to prepare Oats Palak Cheela:
  1. You need oats flour
  2. Prepare rice flour
  3. Take semolina
  4. Use spinach purée
  5. Take cummin seeds
  6. You need red chilli powder
  7. Use curds
  8. Prepare Salt
  9. Get water
  10. Take Oil for shallow fry
Instructions to make Oats Palak Cheela:
  1. Grind oats in mixer to make oats flour.
  2. In a bowl take all three types of flours mentioned
  3. Add cumin seeds and purée to it - Add curd and give a nice mix to form a smooth batter.
  4. If you want can add chopped onions too. - Now keep the batter aside for 10-15 min
  5. Heat a non-stick tawa. Grease with oil.
  6. Pour a big serving spoon of prepared batter. - Spread in circular motion but not too big as a dosa.
  7. Sprinkle little oil. Cook for 3-4 minutes on medium flame.
  8. Flip and cook it for another 2-3 minutes or till done.
  9. Take it out in a plate and serve hot with tomato ketchup or any chutney of your liking.
