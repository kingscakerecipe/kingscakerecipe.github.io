The ingredients needed to cook Oatmeal porridge topped with berries and raisins:
  1. Prepare Quick rolled plain oats
  2. Get Approx Milk
  3. Get Dried berries
  4. Provide Cardomom /Cinnamon powder
  5. Prepare Sugar/Jaggery
Steps to make Oatmeal porridge topped with berries and raisins:
  1. Dry roast the rolled oats until little golden and aromatic. Set aside.
  2. Boil milk in a pan, add in the oats, let them cook for 3 to 5 mins.
  3. When it thickens add in the sugar or jaggery followed by cinnamon powder. Cook for half a min more.
  4. Serve warm, I topped it up with dries berries and raisins, you may use nuts or fresh fruits like bananas or strawberries. ENJOY !!!
