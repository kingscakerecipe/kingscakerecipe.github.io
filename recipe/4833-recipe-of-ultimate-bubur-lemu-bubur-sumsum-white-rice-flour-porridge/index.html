The ingredients needed to make Bubur Lemu / Bubur Sumsum / White Rice Flour Porridge:
  1. Prepare 250 gram plain white rice flour
  2. Prepare 200 ml water
  3. Prepare 300 ml water
  4. Take Pinch salt
  5. Provide 2 pandan leaves tie into a knot
  6. Provide 5 cubes palm sugar54
  7. Prepare 200 ml more water
Instructions to make Bubur Lemu / Bubur Sumsum / White Rice Flour Porridge:
  1. Mix the flour, salt and the coconut milk together until smooth and well combined.
  2. Put the water into a pan, add one of the pandan leaves, bring to a boil. Then add the flour mixture gradually, while stirring until the mixture thickens and smooth and cooked through. Put off the heat.
  3. For the sauce: put some water into a pan, add the pal sugar, pandan leaf and salt. Bring to a boil. Put off the heat.
  4. Serve the porridge with the sauce.
