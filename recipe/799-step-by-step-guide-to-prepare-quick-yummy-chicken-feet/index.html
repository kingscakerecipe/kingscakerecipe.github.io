The ingredients needed to prepare Yummy Chicken feet:
  1. Provide Chicken feet
  2. Get Water for boiling
  3. You need Canola cooking oil
  4. Get Onions
  5. You need Crushed garling
  6. Take cubes Stock
  7. Take Robertson mix herb
  8. Provide Cayenne pepper
  9. Get Paprika powder
  10. Use Cummin powder
  11. Prepare Spice shotgun grill seasoning
  12. Get Black pepper
  13. You need Tomato paste
Steps to make Yummy Chicken feet:
  1. Wash chicken feet
  2. Boil chicken feet for about 25-30 minutes
  3. Remove chicken feet from stove once cooked and soft. Drain water and put aside.
  4. Heat oil on a separate pan.
  5. Add finely chopped onion and crushed garlic. Stir for a minute or two.
  6. Add all the spice(all the ingredients except paste), one at a time according to your preference. Stir and let the spice cook for a minute.
  7. Add chicken feet, stir. Close the pot for five minutes.
  8. Add tomato paste and stir again. Turn the stove to low heart.
  9. Leave it for another three minutes.
  10. Eat it as a snack or a side to your main dish.
